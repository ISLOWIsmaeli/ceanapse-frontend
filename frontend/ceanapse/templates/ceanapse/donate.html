Lacustrine Community Park

Restoring Lake Victoria's shoreline through youth-led action

Shauri Yako, an informal settlement on the edge of Lake Victoria, has been plagued by pollution, waste dumping, and shoreline degradation. Ceanapse is bringing together young people to reclaim this neglected lakeside and transform it into the first ever Lacustrine Community Park in Homabay (a clean, green, community-owned space that protects the lake and restores dignity).

What Your Support Builds:

  • Shoreline Restoration Zone: Bamboo, vetiver grass, and native trees to stop erosion and revive biodiversity
  • Community Green Park: Safe, accessible park with seating, shade, and child friendly spaces from recycled materials
  • Waste Management Hub: Youth managed sorting points and community sensitization
  • Micro Urban Farming: Aquaponics units for food security and income generation
  • Environmental Education Space: Open air learning area for conservation and climate workshops
Status: Fully Youth-Led
Community: Shauri Yako, Homabay
